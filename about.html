About Us

Vault is a place where the Whitewater community can build a healthier, stronger, and lasting body through activities and a stimulating environment. Bonding and having fun in a safe and friendly environment is our main concern.Vault is a stress-reducing environment where students and faculty can get out all of their negative emotions. It also promotes a more creative way to stay healthy, active, and adventurous where individuals can become a part of a more unique society and community.
